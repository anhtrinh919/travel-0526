# Tuy Hòa → Quy Nhơn → Đà Nẵng

**15 – 23 May · nine days · motorbike + private car**

---

## Day 1 — Friday, 15 May · Tuy Hòa arrival

- **13:35** Land at Tuy Hòa (TBB). Taxi to hotel.
- **16:00** Tháp Nghinh Phong — basalt columns at golden hour.
- **17:30** City beach, barefoot.
- **19:00** Dinner at Quán Bà Tám (ocean tuna eye).

**Eat / Sip**
- Bánh Canh Hẹ Thành Tùng — Phú Yên chive noodle. Lê Lợi.
- Bánh Mì Chấm Nguyễn Huệ — bread in meatball broth. 94 Nguyễn Huệ.
- Quán Bà Tám — ocean tuna eye. 289 Lê Duẩn.
- Huy Tùng Coffee — heritage Phú Yên house. 125 Nguyễn Trãi.

**Logistics** — Airport taxi (~150k). Everything else walkable.

---

## Day 2 — Saturday, 16 May · South Phú Yên (motorbike)

- **08:00** Mũi Điện lighthouse & Bãi Môn beach.
- **12:00** Lunch on a floating raft, Vũng Rô Bay.
- **13:30** Boat out to Hòn Nưa island.
- **16:30** Coastal sunset ride home.

**Eat**
- Bè nổi Vi Anh / Thanh Niên — Vũng Rô floating rafts, live seafood from cages.
- Cơm Niêu Năm Ánh — claypot rice. 394 Hùng Vương.
- Nem Nướng Nhật Hoàng — grilled pork rolls. 04 Trần Bình Trọng.
- Koi Cafe — fish-pond space. An Dương Vương.

**Logistics** — Mũi Điện alone. Hòn Nưa by local boat at Vũng Rô pier (150–200k/person, no agency).

---

## Day 3 — Sunday, 17 May · North Phú Yên (motorbike)

- **08:30** Hòn Yến — low-tide coral garden.
- **12:00** Lunch at Đầm Ô Loan.
- **15:30** Bãi Xép cliffs — *Tôi thấy hoa vàng trên cỏ xanh* filming location.

**Eat**
- Quán Tuấn / Nhà hàng Thúy Kiều — Ô Loan lagoon. Blood cockles, steamed grouper.
- Bún cá Đất Phú — light fish noodle soup. 169 Lê Thánh Tôn.
- Cháo Hàu 373 — oyster porridge. 373 Nguyễn Huệ.
- Alice — Tea Room — garden villa cafe.

**Logistics** — Alone, Google Maps does it all.

---

## Day 4 — Monday, 18 May · Transfer to Quy Nhơn (private car)

- **09:00** Gành Đá Đĩa — honeycomb basalt columns.
- **11:30** QL1D scenic drive; stop at Vịnh Hòa beach.
- **~13:00** Lunch at Sông Cầu (lobster capital).
- **15:00** Arrive Quy Nhơn, check in.

**Eat**
- Quán Hải Sản Gành Đỏ — Sông Cầu halfway stop.
- Bếp Nhà Xứ Nẫu — garden-home dining. 68 Nam Cao. 4.7★.
- Lẩu Cua Ông Minh — crab hotpot. 29 Diên Hồng.
- Surf Bar — iconic beach cafe, sunset.

**Logistics** — **Tuy Hòa GO** for the transfer (transparent prices, driver doubles as mini-guide). ~1.2–1.5M VND for the day.

---

## Day 5 — Tuesday, 19 May · Hòn Khô (motorbike)

- **08:00** Ride to Nhơn Hải Village.
- **09:00** Canoe, snorkel, walk the tidal stone bridge at Hòn Khô.
- **12:00** Village lunch — snails, squid, fresh fish.
- **13:30** Ride back to the city. Quiet afternoon.
- Early night.

**Eat**
- Hương Dương Seafood — Nhơn Hải village favourite.
- Bún cá Ngọc Liên — 379 Nguyễn Huệ. 4,000+ reviews.
- Gia Vỹ 2 — bánh xèo tôm nhảy. 14 Diên Hồng.
- Adiuvat Coffee Roasters — specialty beans. Nguyễn Huệ.

**Logistics**
- Hòn Khô **pier tour** at Nhơn Hải — ~350k VND, includes canoe, snorkel gear, seafood lunch.
- Ask for child-sized life jacket (*áo phao trẻ em*) before boarding.
- Negotiate a 2-day bike rate — Day 6 needs it too.

---

## Day 6 — Wednesday, 20 May · A slow day, then the stars

- **Morning** Sleep in. Pool, city beach, no agenda.
- **14:00** Ride south to Bãi Xép Village, Ghềnh Ráng.
- **16:30** Afternoon drink by the cove.
- **18:00** ExploraScience — 7 theme rooms + Science Show.
- **19:00 – 21:00** **Observatory** — Wed-night stargazing session.

**Eat / Sip**
- Light lunch near the hotel.
- Sunset drink — Surf Bar or Bãi Xép Village.
- On-site café at ExploraScience between museum and observatory.

**Logistics**
- Book ExploraScience online: **ticket.explorascience.vn** (~120–150k/person).
- Observatory runs Wednesday & Saturday nights **only**. Cancelled if overcast — have a backup dinner plan.
- Bike south in the afternoon; Grab back if tired.

---

## Day 7 — Thursday, 21 May · All day at Trung Lương

- **09:30** Ride north to Trung Lương Outdoor Area.
- **12:00** Lunch at Cát Tiến — Nhà hàng Cánh Quạt under the wind turbines.
- **13:30** Back to Trung Lương. Nap, swim, repeat.
- **16:30** Ride home via **Thị Nại Bridge** at sunset.

**Eat**
- Nhà hàng Cánh Quạt — airy seafood, Cát Tiến.
- Any street stall back in the city — you'll be salty and tired.
- Marina Coffee — if you've got energy left. 05 Đô Đốc Bảo.

**Logistics**
- Trung Lương entrance fee (~50k/person).
- Last motorbike day — return the rental by evening.
- Stop halfway across Thị Nại Bridge for the lagoon sunset.

---

## Day 8 — Friday, 22 May · Quy Nhơn → Đà Nẵng

- **Morning** Transfer (~6 hours via QL1).
- **Afternoon** Check in, find water, walk Mỹ Khê beach.
- **Evening** No plans. Seafood near the hotel.

**Logistics** — Private car, open-bus, or train. Book the morning slot so the afternoon is free.

---

## Day 9 — Saturday, 23 May · Departure

- **Morning** Beach walk. One last cà phê.
- **Afternoon** Taxi to DAD, fly to Hà Nội.

**Logistics** — Hotel transfer or Grab. DAD is 15 minutes from Mỹ Khê. Terminal 1 for domestic.

---

## Tour operators (Vietnamese-friendly, fair-priced)

**Phú Yên**
- **Tuy Hòa GO** (tuyhoago.com) — car rentals & private transfers. Transparent pricing.
- **Hello Phú Yên** (hellophuyen.vn) — off-the-beaten-path local tours.

**Quy Nhơn**
- **Quy Nhơn Group Tour** — operates the Nhơn Hải pier canoes for Hòn Khô.
- **Vương Khang Travel** — family-friendly village tours.
